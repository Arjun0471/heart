About Dr. Swati Garekar


Dr. Swati Garekar is a pediatric cardiologist based in Mumbai/Thane, India.
She completed her MBBS at Seth GS Medical College/KEM Hospital, Mumbai.
Her pediatrics residency (3 years) was followed by Pediatric Cardiology Fellowship (3 years) at the Children's Hospital Of Michigan, Detroit, Michigan, United States.
She maintains an active license from the American Board of Pediatrics (sub speciality of Pediatric Cardiology) to date.

She began her career in pediatric cardiology at Wockhardt hospital, Mulund, Mumbai (now know as Fortis Hospital, Mulund, Mumbai) in September 2006. Roped in by like minded senior cardiovascular surgeon, Dr Suresh Joshi, together they started the first dedicated pediatric cardiac centre in Mumbai, offering 24 hour high end care in this speciality for affected families, who till then had to rush to Bangalore. Aside from a brief period of 3 years where she was a consultant pediatric cardiologist at KDA Hospital, Andheri, Mumbai, she has been at Fortis Hospital, Mulund, Mumbai.

Her areas of interest include fetal echocardiography, heart failure, heart transplant and 3D printing of heart defects. She has multiple publications in indexed journals.
